Daym Solution

Price: 1000 Riyal

This robust device integrates sensors to measure:

  • Soil Humidity (%): Measures the volumetric water content of the soil.
  • NPK (ppm): Quantifies the concentration of Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) in parts per million.
  • Soil Temperature (°C/°F): Monitors the temperature of the soil environment
  • Electrical Conductivity (µS/cm or mS/cm): Measures the electrical conductivity of the soil solution, indicating salinity and nutrient availability.
