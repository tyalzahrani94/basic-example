About SmartieeFarm

Our Story

SmartieeFarm is a Saudi-based technology company dedicated to transforming agriculture through the power of smart farming solutions. Our mission is to bridge traditional agricultural practices with advanced IoT technologies to enable data-driven decision-making for farmers, agronomists, and agricultural investors. Backed by a team of skilled Saudi engineers and developers, we are committed to building sustainable, intelligent farming ecosystems that improve crop yield, optimize resource usage, and reduce operational costs.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of smart agricultural systems in the MENA region by empowering local farmers with real-time data, intelligent insights, and innovative tools to enhance food security and environmental sustainability.

Our Values

  • Quality: We are uncompromising in our commitment to providing the freshest and highest quality produce.
  • Sustainability: We strive to employ environmentally responsible farming methods to protect our land for future generations.
  • Saudi Pride: We are a 100% Saudi-owned and operated company, dedicated to showcasing the talent and potential of our people.
  • Community: We believe in building strong relationships with our customers and contributing positively to our local community.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek innovative ways to improve our farming practices and deliver the best products.

Aligned with Saudi Vision 2030

SmartieeFarm supports Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 through:

  • Advancing the digitization of agriculture
  • Reducing water and resource consumption in farming
  • Increasing local food production efficiency
  • Creating high-value jobs for Saudi youth in the tech and agri sectors
  • Enabling national food security through technology-driven precision agriculture
